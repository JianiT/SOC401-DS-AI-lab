Main concerns of the CCTV are privacy issues and if it is worth spending the funds and efforts on the CCTV. 

Police: CCTV help identify and record more crimes. Police would be easier to get more evidence and information for crimes and ensure the safety of the area.

Citizen: CCTV would be a monitor to protect people from minor crimes and find criminals for the crimes has been done. Citizens can expect a better living environment with less worried about their safety. CCTV around the living area is also a potential risk for personal privacy. Citizens living near the areas would need to be careful about being recorded of their behaviors in their neighborhood.

Homeless: Homeless would be under surveillance for the whole day if they do not move around. It is a privacy concern for the homelessness about living under the camera.
