# How can we use CCTVs to control the crime rate in Seattle? 


## Executive Summary

The crime rate was consistently high in the downtown area and international district for the past few years. Addressing this problem is the key point of improving the crime rate in Seattle. City of Seattle want to reduce the crime rate of Seattle by installing CCTVs on the certain area with high crime rate.
